About Us

Our Mission

All Saints Church is a vibrant community of believers committed to glorifying God through worship, fellowship, and service.

Founded in 1978, we have been serving our community for over four decades. Our church family welcomes people from all walks of life to experience God's love and grace.

Our Values

  • Faith in Jesus Christ
  • Community and Fellowship
  • Service to Others
  • Biblical Teaching
